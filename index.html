团体介绍:
‍
东方不败(DFBB)舞团成立于2016年，是CYPN的街舞社团，由一群来自波士顿地区各个高校、各行各业的舞蹈热爱者组成。舞团多变，以Hip-Hop, Jazz, Urban, K-Pop为主，其它舞种也有涉猎。DFBB舞团旨在以舞会友，舞团不定期在波士顿举办OpenPractice/Workshop，希望能让爱好跳舞的小伙伴们有机会来结交朋友交流学习。记得订阅公众号dfbbdance及时获得舞团相关信息哦~ DFBB (DongFangBuBai) is a co-ed dance crew formed under Chinese Young Professionals Network (CYPN), a 503c non-profit organization dedicated to the Chinese population in North America. DFBB does dance choreographies in various styles, with a focus on Hip-Hop, Jazz, Urban and K-Pop. The dancers are students/professionals from various backgrounds with one shared goal: enjoy dancing, dominate on stage, be the best!
