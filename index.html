DFBB does dance choreographies in various styles, with a focus on Hip-Hop, Jazz, Urban and K-Pop. The dancers are students/professionals from various backgrounds with one shared goal: enjoy dancing, dominate on stage, be the best!

舒翼介绍:
‍
魔术是一个既神秘又吸引人的名词，魔术师在舞台 上的变化总是让人捉摸不透。能让有的东西变消 失，更能将没有的东西凭空变出来，还能读透观众 的所思所想。从舞台上的道具，到魔术师，魔术师 的助手，到每一个出现在舞台上的人物，犹如魔法 一般总能创造意想不到的惊喜。这种神秘的艺术深 深地吸引着每一位观众，更让我愿意花费大量的时 间和精力钻研，去成为观众眼中那充满魔法的表演 者。 从中学开始，我正式入门学习专业魔术。完美惊艳 的表演需要日复一日的努力练习，也需要关键时刻 的灵光一现。在这段期间里，也非常感谢我身边的 人给予我很多表演机会，很多很大的舞台，更感谢 我家里人的支持。每一次成功的魔术表演，都离不 开台前台后各种人的努力工作。自从出国留学，我 也认识了许多在国际上优秀的年轻魔术师。在未 来，我希望继续进步，成为他们的一员并联合推动 魔术艺术的进步。 Magic is a mysterious and attractive term. The magician's move on the stage is always elusive: some things can disappear, more things can be conjured out of thin air, and they can even read the audience's thoughts. From the props to the magician himself, the assistant, to every character who appears on the stage, magic can always create surprises. This mysterious art deeply attracts every audience, and also intrigued me to spend plenty of time to research and become a magical performer in the eyes of all audience. I started learning professional magic from very young. I think perfect and stunning performance is the combination of day-to-day hard practice and the moment of brilliance. During these years, I am very grateful to the people around me for giving me a lot of performance opportunities. I also thank my family for their support for me to perusing this art. While studying abroad, I have also met so many outstanding young magicians who are performing on international platforms. This motivates me a lot. In the future, I want to keep my hardworking and advance the progress of magical art with all my friends.
